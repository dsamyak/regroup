# 1. Executive Summary

Regrouping Quest is a fully gamified, simulation-based educational web experience built for Grade 3 students (ages 8–9) worldwide.

It teaches the critical math concept of **Regrouping** — the process of carrying and borrowing in multi-digit addition and subtraction — through an immersive story-driven platform inspired by the Intellia SG Equal Groups module.

## Core Value Proposition

- Students learn by **doing**
- Interactive dragging, clicking, and manipulation of digit blocks
- Guided by four characters:
  - John (USA)
  - Mike (UK)
  - Sarah (Australia)
  - Priya (India)
- Randomized question generation
- Aligned with global curricula
- Audio narration, animations, and rewards

# 2. Product Vision & Goals

## 2.1 Vision Statement

To make regrouping in arithmetic intuitive, memorable, and fun for every 8-year-old on the planet through simulation-based gameplay.

## 2.2 Business Goals

- Increase engagement time by 60%
- Achieve ≥85% concept mastery
- Enable self-learning with ≥80% accuracy
- Position Intellia SG as a leader in gamified Singapore-method math
- Support 50,000+ concurrent users globally

## 2.3 Learning Objectives

By completing Regrouping Quest, students will:

1. Understand regrouping using visual models
2. Add 2-digit and 3-digit numbers with carrying
3. Subtract 2-digit and 3-digit numbers with borrowing
4. Identify when regrouping is required
5. Solve real-world word problems
6. Achieve ≥80% accuracy in randomized practice
